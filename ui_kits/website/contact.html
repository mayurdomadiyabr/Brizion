Orders & Shipping
When will my order ship?

Orders placed before 2 PM ET Monday–Friday ship the same business day from our Brooklyn warehouse. Weekend orders ship Monday morning. You'll get a tracking number by email within 2 hours of shipment.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — to 14 countries. Shipping takes 5–10 business days and customs/duties are calculated at checkout. We don't currently ship retinoids to countries where they require prescription (check a full list on our shipping page).

My package says delivered but I can't find it.

First, check with neighbors and building mailrooms. If it's been 48 hours with no sign, email us with your order number and we'll file a carrier claim and reship at no charge.

Returns & Refunds
What's your return policy?

30 days from delivery on any product — opened or unopened. We cover the return shipping. Refunds land on your original payment method within 5 business days of our warehouse receiving the return.

I had a reaction. What now?

Stop using immediately and email us with a photo if possible. We'll refund in full, talk you through what might have caused it, and — if you want — recommend a gentler alternative from the line.

Can I return a subscription order?

Yes, same 30-day policy. You can also pause or cancel the subscription itself from your account at any time, no questions asked.

Subscriptions
How do I pause or cancel?

Open your account, tap Subscriptions, and use the Pause or Cancel button on any active subscription. No phone call, no retention script, no friction.

Can I change the delivery frequency?

Yes. Every subscription lets you choose between 30, 45, 60, or 90-day cycles. You can change it as often as you like from your account.

What's the subscription discount?

15% off every delivery, forever. First-time subscribers also get free shipping and a travel-size sample with their first box.

Product & Skin
Can I use the serum while pregnant?

The Firm & Lift Serum contains retinal, which is not recommended during pregnancy or breastfeeding. Our Bakuchiol Concentrate is a pregnancy-safe alternative with similar benefits. Always check with your OBGYN first.

In what order should I apply your products?

Thinnest to thickest: cleanser → serum → oil (optional) → moisturizer. In the AM, finish with SPF. In the PM, the Overnight Renewal Cream is the last step.
