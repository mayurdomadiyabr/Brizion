Our hero actives.

Six ingredients do most of the work across the Brizion line. Here's what each does, and how much is in the bottle.

01 · Retinoid

Retinal (the stable one)

Accelerates cell turnover ~10× faster than retinol with less irritation. Our hero anti-aging active.

Concentration0.3%
02 · Peptide

Copper Tripeptide-1 (GHK-Cu)

Stimulates collagen synthesis. Clinically proven to firm skin over 8 weeks of daily use.

Concentration2.0%
03 · Vitamin B3

Niacinamide (not trace)

Evens tone, reduces pore appearance, strengthens the barrier. We use it at efficacy levels.

Concentration5.0%
04 · Plant retinoid

Bakuchiol (retinoid alt.)

Gentle retinoid-like effects for sensitive or pregnant skin. Stacks well with retinal.

Concentration1.0%
05 · Humectant

Hyaluronic Acid (3 MW)

Three molecular weights for hydration at three skin depths. Plumps without sitting on top.

Concentration2.0%
06 · Antioxidant

Vitamin E (Tocopherol)

Neutralizes free radicals, stabilizes the formula, supports the barrier. In every product.

Concentration0.5%

How every product gets made.

01

Research & formulation

Our team of cosmetic chemists works from clinical literature outward. Every active has to justify its concentration with peer-reviewed studies before it enters a formulation.

~ 6 months
02

Lab prototyping

40–60 iterations of each product in our Brooklyn lab. We test stability, pH, viscosity, and sensorial across humidity and temperature ranges.

~ 8 months
03

Independent clinical

Third-party consumer studies with minimum 80 participants, run for 8 weeks. Dermatologist-graded and self-assessed. Results published in full.

~ 8 weeks
04

Derm & safety review

Dr. Sam Okafor and two external dermatologists sign off on the clinical data, safety file, and label before production.

~ 3 weeks
05

Small-batch production

Manufactured in FDA-registered facilities in the US. 6-week expiration dated, refrigerated shipping on actives.

Ongoing
