Our Story · Est. 2021

Skincare, but honest.

Brizion was founded on a simple idea — that great skincare should be backed by clinical proof, not marketing. No hype. No hidden fillers. Just results you can see in the mirror.

"We formulate at concentrations that actually perform. Everything else is noise."— Dr. Mira Chen, Founder

The Manifesto

We believe in results over rhetoric. In dermatologist-formulated actives at concentrations that actually do the work.

We believe your skin should feel like it did yesterday, only better. That's the benchmark we test every formula against.

We don't do trend-chasing. We don't do fear-selling. We do real chemistry, real studies, and real honesty about what a bottle can and can't do.

Three things we don't compromise on.

01

Clinical proof.

Every hero product goes through independent 8-week consumer studies with at least 80 participants before launch. Results get published on the PDP — even the ones we'd rather bury.

02

Honest labels.

What's on the front of the bottle is what's inside. Concentrations are disclosed. The INCI list is posted on every PDP. If an ingredient is trace, we say so.

03

Ritual over routine.

Fewer products, used longer. We formulate multitaskers so you can do less — and we'd rather sell you one bottle that works than twelve that don't.
